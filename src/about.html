About Me

I'm a UI/UX Designer + Developer who loves to design and code modern interfaces. I've been studying design and development since 2014, and have both a Bachelor's in Visual Communication Technology and an Associate's in Web Design. I was born and raised in Northwest Ohio, and I moved to Portland, Oregon in 2021. I'm passionate about providing best-in-class user interface, no matter the industry or application. I love coffee, sushi, music, technology, crypto/blockchain, gaming, Japan, fashion, standup comedy and more. Over time, I hope to help make the world a better place and help solve problems that the world faces via my expertise as a creative technologist.
