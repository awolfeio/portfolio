Rowmark is the leading U.S. manufacturer of laser and rotary engravable sheet plastic. Their engravable materials are used worldwide for many kinds of products, from desk and wall nameplates to glow-in-the-dark illuminated wayfinding signage.

They were greatly in need of a more modern website that showcased their product offerings, worldwide distributors and resources. In 2018, while I was an intern, I completed both the UI/UX design as well as the HTML/SCSS.
